# Taste Profile

## Evidence

- Mira likes warm interfaces with visible craft, specific motion, and emotional texture.
- Mira rejects generic dashboard polish when it has no real point of view.
- The Lumen Sketch export should feel tactile, emotional, useful, and not embarrassed by being pretty.
- The anti-pattern is fake onboarding copy trying to explain what the tool should have made obvious.

## Anti-Taste

- Productivity theater.
- Feature lists that do not change the user’s feeling or artifact.
- Visual polish with no proof moment underneath it.
