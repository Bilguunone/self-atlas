# Pressure and Craft

## What We Know

- Mira focuses better when pressure has a visible artifact attached to it.
- Ten directions can become a hiding place when one finished artifact would expose the taste judgment.
- The good pressure is not panic. It is the moment where the work has to become real enough to save, show, or use.

- What kind of deadline or proof moment creates momentum without turning the work dead?
